------------------------------------------------------------------------
I used two criteria to rank the mystery boards in order of difficulty:

  -The number of steps the program takes to solve the board;
  
  -The time in milliseconds the program takes to solve the board
   (importing the library chrono).
------------------------------------------------------------------------
I found that:

  -Mystery1 is the harder board, with 295 steps and 38 milliseconds
  (on average) to reach a solution;
  
  -Mystery2 is impossible to solve;
  
  -Mystery3 is the easier board, with 270 steps and 13 milliseconds
  (on average) to reach a solution.
